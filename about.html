About MIS

Mastermind International school has been serving the nation since the year 2000. MIS has a large, green campus in the heart of Chittagong. The campus consists of three buildings housing 50 classrooms. We strive to make our students active and enthusiastic participants in school and community life. MIS is offering a sophisticated environment and most highly credential education facilities and added impetus. MIS is also dedicated to developing the ethical and social values of the students. There are many good reasons for you to envisage the extensive options offered by MIS. We sincerely hope that we will have the chance to welcome you as part of MIS family and allow you to enjoy the benefits of the finest learning environments available.

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

To inspire and empower every student to reach their full potential and become compassionate, creative and confident individuals who contribute meaningfully to a global society.

Our Mission

At Mastermind International School, our mission is to deliver holistic education through academic excellence, innovation and values-based learning. nurturing young minds to think critically, lead with integrity and act with empathy.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold honesty, responsibility and strong moral principles in all we do.

Innovation

We encourage curiosity, creativity and forward thinking ideas to solve real world problems.

Global Citizenship

We embrace diversity and prepare students to thrive in a connected global world.

Respect

We treat everyone with kindness, empathy and understanding which builds a strong community.

Lifelong Learning

We instill a love for learning that continues far beyond the classroom.

Critical Thinking

We equip students to question, evaluate and think independently in a complex world.
